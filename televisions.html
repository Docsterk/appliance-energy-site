TV Energy Usage Guide

Televisions can vary significantly in their energy consumption based on technology, size, and usage patterns.

TV Technology Comparison

Technology Average Power Consumption Energy Efficiency
LED/LCD 50-100W High
OLED 60-120W Medium-High
QLED 70-150W Medium
Plasma (older models) 150-300W Low

Energy Saving Tips for TVs

  • Choose the right size - larger screens consume more energy
  • Look for TVs with high energy star ratings
  • Enable power-saving modes
  • Turn off completely when not in use (avoid standby mode)
  • Adjust brightness settings - lower brightness uses less energy
